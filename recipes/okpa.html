Okpa nsukka

Description

Okpa is special type of igbo food that is made with grain beans. It is a student saviour in UNN. It can be pap, garri, moimoi etc

Ingredients

  • Powder grain beans
  • Paim oil
  • Pepper
  • Uzuza
  • Salt
  • Hot water

Steps

  1. Pour some quantity of powdered grain beans inside a bowel add all ingredients
  2. Add palm oil and turn it with hot water untill it is watery
  3. Tie it inside white leather and put back inside pot with hot water
  4. Boil untill it is congil
