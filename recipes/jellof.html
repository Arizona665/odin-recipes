To jellof process

Jellof rice

Description

This Nigerian jellof rice were made in last african jrllof rice competion. it is well spiced and delicious

Ingredients

  • Groundnut oil
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Maggi
  • Green beans
  • Crafish
  • Fish
  • Pepper
  • Fresh tomatos

STEPS

  1. Paboil rice for 5mins
  2. Use a clearn pot and add some quantity of groundnut oil inside the pot and warm it
  3. Add onions plus tomatos plus salt then fry it with the oil till it dehydrated then add two cups of water
  4. Add pepper+crafish+salt+maggi+fish then turn it
  5. Finally, add the paboiled rice inside the stew and make sure the water is at the brim and boil till water dry
