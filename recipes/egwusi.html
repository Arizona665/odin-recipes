Eggusi soap

Description

This soap is made with medicinal vegetable and well spiced. It nomally eaten as swallow with garri, akpu, pounded yam etc

Ingredients

  • Grinded egwsi
  • Okpeye
  • Palm oil
  • Crafish
  • Vegetable
  • Salt
  • Magara

Steps

  1. Mix egwusi with palm oil thorouly and put inside pot containing hot water heart for 10 mins
  2. Remove the egusi from the pot and break it then put it back
  3. Add all the remaning ingredients
  4. Heat for 15mins then put down
  5. Soap is ready
